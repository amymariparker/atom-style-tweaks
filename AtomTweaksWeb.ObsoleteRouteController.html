    long_tweak_path_to_short(Plug.Conn.t(), Map.t()) :: Plug.Conn.t()

    Redirects the old-style long tweak paths to the new shorter ones.

    For example: /users/foo/tweaks/df15c988-f262-4c03-bb73-dcfd4a8161ee becomes /tweaks/df15c988-f262-4c03-bb73-dcfd4a8161ee.
